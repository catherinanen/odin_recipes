Golonka (Pork Knuckle)

What is this?

Golonka is a traditional Polish dish consisting of pork knuckle or hock that is typically braised or roasted. The meat is often seasoned with garlic, marjoram, and bay leaves, and served with sauerkraut, potatoes, or bread. This hearty dish is known for its rich flavor and tender, melt-in-your-mouth texture.

Fun Fact: Best dish in polish cusine ~ Mitsou

Ingredients:

  • 1 or 2 Pork Knuckle
  • 1l of broth
  • 50g of butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic
